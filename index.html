Are you a data scientist looking for new and interesting data sets or ways to apply your skills?
Do you need help applying data science techniques to your work to improve your outcomes or the impact of your science?

If you said yes to either of these questions, join us on November 18, 2022 at the [insert campus location here] for Oregon Data Science Day. This day-long workshop and networking event will bring together scientists from across Oregon to learn about data science resources in Oregon, network with potential colleagues, and help inform our efforts to build community and offer training and consulting services to promote data science in Oregon. We will host 3 sessions during the day focused on: Environmental science, health and biomedical sciences, and engineering. The workshop will consist of brief 10 min flash talk by participants followed by discussion and networking for each of the subject areas. There will also be time set aside for community input on the services and trainings that are more desirable to scientists in order to promote the broader use of data science in Oregon.
